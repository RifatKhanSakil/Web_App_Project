# Interviews

## Interview 1: Farm Owner

Findings:
- Needs better marketing.
- Wants online visibility.
- Requires organized livestock records.

## Interview 2: Customer

Findings:
- Wants to see cattle details before visiting.
- Prefers viewing animal images.
- Needs pricing information.

## Interview 3: Local Buyer

Findings:
- Interested in bulk purchases.
- Requires quick communication with farm owners.
