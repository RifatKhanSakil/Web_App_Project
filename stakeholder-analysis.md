# Stakeholder Analysis

## Farm Owner

Responsibilities:
- Manage livestock information.
- Update available cattle and goats.
- Communicate with customers.

## Customers

Responsibilities:
- Browse livestock.
- Request information.
- Purchase animals.

## Farm Employees

Responsibilities:
- Assist in animal management.
- Maintain livestock records.

## Local Market Buyers

Responsibilities:
- Purchase livestock in bulk.
- Communicate market demand.

## Future Investors

Responsibilities:
- Evaluate farm growth opportunities.
