# Project Overview

## Project Name
Cattle Farm Management System

## Project Description

The Cattle Farm Management System is a web-based platform designed to help a cattle and goat farm showcase available livestock and facilitate sales. The system allows customers to browse cattle and goats, view animal details, and contact the farm for purchasing. The platform also supports farm promotion, especially during the Eid-ul-Adha season, while maintaining year-round sales to local markets and individual buyers.

## Objectives

- Digitize farm operations.
- Promote cattle and goat sales online.
- Increase visibility during Eid-ul-Adha.
- Provide detailed livestock information to buyers.
- Build customer trust through transparency.

## Expected Outcomes

- Improved customer engagement.
- Increased livestock sales.
- Better farm branding.
- Easier access to animal information.
- Future readiness for online booking and payment systems.
